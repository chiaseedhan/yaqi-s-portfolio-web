About

Yaqi
Han

Yaqi Han is an illustrator and graphic designer based in Edinburgh, originally from Beijing. Her practice spans graphic novel, branding, digital design, and editorial illustration, with a focus on visual storytelling, emotion, and the quiet details of everyday life. Working across both personal and client-led projects, she creates thoughtful, playful visuals that balance narrative sensitivity with clear communication.

Her recent work includes projects for the University of Edinburgh, Tencent Video, and Vortex Game AI, as well as selected work presented through Secret 7”, the Edinburgh International Book Festival, and other exhibition contexts.
